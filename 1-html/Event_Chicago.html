About Milky Chance:

Milky Chance is a German folk group originating from Kassel. It consists of lead vocalist and guitarist Clemens Rehbein, DJ and producer Philipp Dausch, and guitarist Antonio Greger. Their first single "Stolen Dance" was released in April 2013, peaking at No. 1 on charts in multiple countries including Austria, Belgium (Wallonia), Czech Republic, France, Hungary, Poland, and Switzerland.[1] It also won the 1Live Krone radio awards for Best Single.

Current members:

  • Clemens Rehbein (2012–present) – lead vocals, guitar, percussion
  • Philipp Dausch (2012–present) – Digital audio workstation, turntables, percussion, backing vocals, guitar, bass
  • Antonio Greger (2015–present) – guitar, harmonica, bass
